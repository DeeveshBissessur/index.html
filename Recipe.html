Deevesh's Sponge Cake Recipe

For the Ingredients:

  • 4 Eggs
  • 100g Flour
  • 100g Sugar
  • Vanilla Essence

Procedure:

  1. Preheat Oven to 200 degrees celsius for 10 minutes
  2. In a bowl, add the eggs and sugar
  3. Whisk together the eggs and sugar mixture until thick and frothy
  4. Add flour little to little using a metal spoon
  5. Mix the mixture a little bit between each flour addition.
  6. Add a little bit of vanilla essence and mix a little bit.
  7. Pour the mixture into a greased container/baking tin and put in the oven for 25 minutes until golden brown
